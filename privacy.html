Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how ZELDIGITAL Philippines ("we", "us") collects, uses, and safeguards information when you visit this site or engage our services. We respect your privacy and comply with applicable Philippine laws. For U.S. entities, we maintain a presence via a Wyoming LLC.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you submit via forms (name, email, company, message).
  • Usage data via analytics (e.g., GA4) such as pages viewed, events, device information, and approximate location.
  • Communications such as emails or meeting notes necessary to deliver services.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide advisory services.
  • To improve site performance and content.
  • To maintain security, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

Legal Bases

We process data based on legitimate interests in operating and improving our services, performance of a contract, and compliance with legal requirements. Where required, we obtain consent (e.g., for marketing communications).

Analytics and Cookies

We use analytics tools such as Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and may deploy tags via Google Tag Manager (GTM), including server‑side tagging. These may use cookies or similar technologies. You can manage cookies in your browser. See Google’s policies for more details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with trusted providers who assist us in operations (e.g., hosting, analytics, scheduling, form processing) under appropriate safeguards, or where required by law.

Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law and contracts.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

International Transfers

Data may be processed in the Philippines, the United States, or other locations of our service providers, subject to appropriate safeguards.

Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.
