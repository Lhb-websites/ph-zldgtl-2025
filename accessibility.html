Accessibility Statement

We are committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability.

Our commitment

  • Strive to meet WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines where feasible.
  • Use semantic HTML, proper labels, color contrast, and keyboard navigability.
  • Continuously improve based on feedback and audits.

Known limitations

Some third‑party components and embedded content may not fully meet accessibility standards. We work with providers to address issues as they arise.
